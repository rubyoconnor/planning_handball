Brief

I have been asked to develop a website for Handball New Zealand. Jerry the President of NZ Handball, has approached me, and we have discussed the key aims for the website. The aims have been listed out in order of priority and this means that I can complete and have feedback on tasks at regular intervals. Before starting the website I completed some UX studies - Looking at the types of users who will be accessing the site and the kind of design and experiences that they expect when they are online - Doing some competition analysis to get a sense of the design style and features of websites that perform similar functions. - Completing a basic plan for the site, with the understanding that this most likely will evolve over the project

Sprint Two

Aim

Finalise design of the homepage

After discussing with the Jerry we decided that the design of the banner above was the best suit for the website. As the photo as the banner was going to be too bold for the website. This is because the purpose of the website is to inform people so it needs to be as clear as possible. Having a big image like this as the banner could be overwhelming for the user and make their experience worse.

Above shows another trial for the homepage. This is because although that banner was the design that was decided on, I continued to make more versions of the homepage following this design. I added the photo to try to give the page more colour but I didn't really like the placement of it. So removed it again.

Above is the final design of the homepage. I changed the events from horizontal to vertical so that the user could see what else is on the page, in order to hopefully make them interested. I added the photos and the lines next to the subtitles in order to add a bit of colour to the page.
